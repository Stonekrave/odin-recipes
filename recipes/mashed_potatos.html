Mashed Potatos

Description

Learn how to make the best mashed potatoes with this easy recipe! Rich, creamy, and flavorful, they're always a crowd-pleasing side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs Russet potatos
  • 1 Cup of whole milk
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1/4 Cup of sour cream

Steps

  1. Peal and cube potatos
  2. Place potatos in a stock with cold water over medium heat
  3. Bring to a boil and simmer until potatos are fork tender
  4. Drain potatos and mash in the stock pot
  5. Add butter and allow to melt
  6. Once butter is melted, add in the milk and sour cream while stirring to combine
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste
