Mississippi Chicken

Description

This 5-ingredient Mississippi chicken recipe is really easy to make and perfectly juicy! The signature pepperoncini ranch sauce tastes zesty and amazing.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 6 pepperoncini peppers
  • 1 packet (1 ounce) aujus gravy
  • 1 packet (1 ounce) ranch seasoning mix
  • 1 stick of butter

Steps

  1. Trim any excess fat from chicken
  2. Place chicken in the bottom of a slow cooker
  3. Sprinkle over au jus and ranch seasoning
  4. Cut butter in 1 Tbsp size pieces and place on top of chicken
  5. Place 6 pepperoncini peppers on top of chicken
  6. Cook on low for 8 hours
  7. Shred chicken and enjoy over mashed potatos
